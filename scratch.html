How to Learn a New Song
  1. Write in your counting
  2. Section it off
    • Music has natural sections where melodies, notes, or keys change
    • Dividing it into sections makes it less overwhelming
  3. Start one hand at a time
  4. Piece it all together
    • Put it hands together
    • Still only work one section at a time
    • After it's hands together, put the sections together
  5. Add dynamics
    • This includes crescendos and diminuendos
    • Use ritardandos and accelerandos
  6. Drill work
    • Work on small sections that are struggle points
  7. Perform
